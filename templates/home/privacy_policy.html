Privacy Policy


This Privacy Policy explains how livingwaterheritage.ca (the “Site” or “we”) collects, uses, and shares your Personal Information when you visit or make a purchase on the Site.

Collecting Personal Information

When you visit the Site, we gather certain details about your device, your interactions with the Site, and information required to process your purchases. Additional data may be collected if you reach out to us for customer support. In this Privacy Policy, any data that uniquely identifies an individual is referred to as “Personal Information.” Below is an outline of the Personal Information we collect and its purposes:

Device Information

Examples: Web browser version, IP address, time zone, cookie information, visited pages, search terms, and site interactions.

Purpose: To display the Site correctly and analyze its usage to improve functionality.

Source: Collected automatically using cookies, log files, web beacons, tags, or pixels.

Disclosure: Shared with our service processor, Shopify.

Order Information

Examples: Name, billing address, shipping address, payment information (e.g., credit card numbers), email address, and phone number.

Purpose: To process orders, manage payments, arrange deliveries, and provide related communications, including invoices or confirmations.

Source: Collected directly from you.

Disclosure: Shared with our service processor, Shopify.

Customer Support Information

Purpose: To assist with inquiries or issues you may encounter.

Source: Collected directly from you.


Minors

The Site is not designed for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect Personal Information from children. If you believe your child has provided such information, please contact us to request its deletion.
