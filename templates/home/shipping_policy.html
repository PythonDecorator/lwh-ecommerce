Shipping Policy


Deliveries are carried out twice a week. Orders are typically delivered within 2 to 5 business days from the date of order placement. Please note that we only deliver within a 20km radius of our locations.

A delivery notification will be sent once payment has been received and confirmed (usually within 24 to 48 hours). Interac payments may require additional processing time of up to 2-3 business days before confirmation is sent.

If the recipient is unavailable at the time of delivery, the order will be left at the front door or another accessible entrance of the address provided during the order.

For special delivery instructions, please contact us at online@livingwaterheritage.com.

Free Shipping

Orders exceeding $150 qualify for free shipping.

Cancellations

Order cancellations cannot be processed once the delivery confirmation has been sent.
